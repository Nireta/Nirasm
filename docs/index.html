FAQ's

1. How do I participate?

Submit a dance video through the official form.(Min 30 sec & Max 120 Sec long)

2. What is the entry fee?

A nominal entry cum trophy & certificate delivery fee of ₹150 is applicable for participation.

3. Can I participate in multiple categories?

Yes, multiple category entries are allowed. Each category requires a separate submission.

4. Will every participant get a trophy & certificate?

Yes, all participants will receive a trophy & certificate delivered to their address. Click here to track consignment.

5. Will I get updates after submitting the form?

Yes, you will receive updates via WhatsApp as per the details you provide.

6. Will my submitted videos be used elsewhere?

Selected entries may be featured on our official website, social media pages, or promotional materials — with proper credit to the owner.

7. How will I know if I’ve won?

Winners will be announced on the official website and notified via WhatsApp and phone call.

About Us










Nira Studio & Media

is a creative platform dedicated to promoting talent, art, and digital expression. We specialize in organizing online contests, producing high-quality creative content, and offering opportunities for artists across various fields to showcase their skills. Our mission is to provide a transparent, engaging, and supportive environment where emerging talents can grow, perform, and gain recognition on a global stage.

Nira Dance Competition 2025

is an online national-level dance contest designed to celebrate creativity, passion, and performance excellence. Participants from all age groups can submit their dance videos across multiple categories and compete for trophy & certificates, awards, and social media features. The competition aims to bring dancers together on one platform, offering a fair, fun, and inspiring opportunity to showcase their talent to a wider audience.
