Guidelines

Read before you submit.
✔

Original Work Only The submitted photograph must be clicked by the participant and should not infringe any copyright.

✔

Allowed Devices Photos can be captured using Mobile, DSLR, SLR, Mirrorless, Point-and-Shoot, Drone.

✔

No AI or Manipulated Images AI-generated or heavily edited/manipulated images will be disqualified.

✔

Editing Limits Only minor edits such as brightness, contrast, cropping, or color correction are allowed.

✔

File Format & Size Images must be in JPEG/JPG/PNG format with a maximum size of 20 MB per photo.

✔

No Watermarks or Logos Submitted photos should not contain any watermark, logo, or text.

✔

Copyright Ownership The photographer retains full rights to their image, but allows Nira to use it for promotional purposes (with proper credit).

✔

Originality Verification Participants must provide proof of originality (RAW file or metadata) if requested by judges.

✔

Offensive Content Prohibited Photos containing violence, nudity, hate, or sensitive content will not be accepted.

✔

Submission Method All photos must be submitted only through the official online form before the deadline.

✔

Judges’ Decision Final All results declared by the judging panel will be final and binding.

FAQ's

1. How many photos can I submit?

Each participant can submit single photo across the allowed categories.

2. Can I participate in more than one category?

Yes, you may submit entries in multiple categories.

3. How will the certificates be delivered?

Certificates will be dispatched to the addresses provided during submission via courier service. Click here to track your certificate.

4. Are winners required to attend the ceremony?

Yes, winners are requested to attend the award distribution ceremony at Ibis Hebbal, Bengaluru.

5. Can I submit previously published photos?

Yes, as long as you own the full rights to the image.

6. Will my submitted photos be used elsewhere?

Selected entries may be featured on our official website, social media pages, or promotional materials — with proper credit to the photographer.

7. How will I know if I’ve won?

Winners will be announced on the official website and notified via email and phone call.
