AI in Healthcare

Diagnostics, Drug Discovery, and Personalized Medicine

Artificial intelligence is playing a revolutionary role in modern healthcare. AI-powered diagnostic tools can detect diseases like cancer or diabetic retinopathy with high accuracy using medical imaging. Meanwhile, AI models are accelerating drug discovery by predicting how new compounds will interact with the body.

AI is also enabling more personalized treatment plans by analyzing vast amounts of patient data to recommend therapies tailored to individual genetic profiles. While AI can't replace doctors, it is rapidly becoming an indispensable tool in improving outcomes and efficiency in healthcare systems.
